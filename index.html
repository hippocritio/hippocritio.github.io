About hippocrit.io

hippocrit.io is a platform dedicated to teaching data analytics, data science, and data engineering with a focus on creating positive social impact. We believe that data has the power to solve some of the world's most pressing challenges.

Our mission is to equip data professionals, researchers, and students with the knowledge and tools needed to work effectively with humanitarian data, climate datasets, health information, and other data sources that can drive meaningful change in the world.
